About Laura:

Laura's Canned Goods is owned and operated by Laura Boyt in Woodville, Texas. All jams, pickles and other canned items are produced using the freshest ingredients in Laura's garden. Laura has several years experience with growing fruits, vegetables and canning. When you order from Laura's Canned Goods, you support a small town one-woman agricultural business. We appreciate your patronage.
